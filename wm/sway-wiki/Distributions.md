If you'd prefer Sway to come pre-installed with a more complete default configuration, you may want to opt for one of the Sway distributions.

- [EndeavourOS Sway Community Edition](https://github.com/EndeavourOS-Community-Editions/sway)
- [Fedora Sway Spin](https://fedoraproject.org/spins/sway/)
- [Manjaro Sway Edition](https://github.com/manjaro-sway/manjaro-sway)
- [openSUSEway](https://github.com/openSUSE/openSUSEway)
- [Regolith DE](https://regolith-desktop.com/)
- [SwayOS](https://swayos.github.io/)
- [Sxmo](https://sxmo.org/)
- [Ubuntu Sway Remix](https://ubuntusway.com/)
